Townsayt Custom Apparel About Townsayt Midsayap

Townsayt Custom Apparel is a premier provider of high-quality, fully sublimated apparel and custom print solutions. With a focus on T-shirts, polo shirts, long-sleeves, sportswear, and other sublimation products, our team has dedicated itself to mastering the art of sublimation printing. After years of rigorous experimentation and refinement, we proudly deliver expertly crafted colors, clean details, and comfortable apparel.

The Machines

We use top-of-the-line Japanese-made machines and genuine inks to support vibrant, durable, and precisely crafted output. Every order is handled with careful color checking, material preparation, and finishing so each piece is ready for daily wear, events, business teams, and sports groups.
